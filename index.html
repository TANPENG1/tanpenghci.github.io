About Me

I’m Peng TAN (檀鹏, Arvin), an HCI researcher at CHEC, Kochi University of Technology, JAPAN. I’m a PhD Candidate of Interdisciplinary Studies in Human-Engaged Computing supervised by Professor Xiangshi REN. Previously I was a Master of Cognition and Interaction Design supervised by Professor Yi JI and Sean Clark in Guangdong University of Technology, China. Before starting my master, I had a bachelor of Industrial Design at Lizhou Institute of Technology.
